Get started

How a jam works.

  1. 01

    Start or join a jam

    Create a room in seconds. Share a secure invite link. Your team joins from any browser — or by SIP/PSTN for dial-in participants. No app install required for guests.

    Start a jam ↗ Join a meeting ↗
  2. 02

    Jam with your AI Jammer

    Your Jammer hops in automatically. It transcribes every word, tracks every decision, flags risks as they emerge, and answers any question you throw at it — mid-meeting, zero friction. Ask it in plain language.

  3. 03

    Walk away with the recap

    When the session wraps, your Jammer hands you a complete write-up: decisions with owners, action items with due dates, risks, highlights, and a full searchable transcript. Download it as Markdown or JSON.
