Get started

How a jam works.

STEP 01

Start or join a jam

Create a room in seconds. Share a secure invite link. Your team joins from any browser — or by SIP / PSTN for dial-in participants. No app install required for guests. Lobby and room lock keep sessions intentional.

STEP 02

Jam with your AI Jammer

Your Jammer hops in automatically. It transcribes every word, tracks every decision, flags risks as they emerge, and answers any question you throw at it — mid-meeting, zero friction. Ask it in plain language.

STEP 03

Walk away with the recap

When the session wraps, your Jammer hands you a complete write-up: decisions with owners, action items with due dates, risks, highlights, and a full searchable transcript. Download as Markdown or JSON.
