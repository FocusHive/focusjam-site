PARTICIPATION

How involved should your Jammer be?

Four presets, one custom dial. Flip between them mid-jam — no restart needed.

Assertiveness 1–2

Watch

Silent observer. Only speaks when summoned by wake phrase or chat command. Perfect for sensitive negotiations or exec reviews.

Assertiveness 3–5

Assist

Jumps in when it genuinely helps — answering questions, flagging decisions, catching action items. Stays out of your way otherwise.

Most popular
Assertiveness 6–8

Cohost

Actively co-runs the jam — keeps you on agenda, calls out risks, summarizes on the fly, and nudges the room when energy dips.

Assertiveness 9–10

Lead

Your Jammer drives the meeting. Opens, guides, timeboxes, and closes — you focus on the conversation, it handles the structure.

Custom

Set the exact assertiveness value (1–10) and choose precisely which triggers are active. Your Jammer, dialed in to the decimal.
