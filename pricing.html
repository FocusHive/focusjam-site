FAQ

Common questions,
straight answers.

Where does my data and AI processing run?

FocusJam operates its own AI infrastructure — no third-party AI SaaS (no OpenAI, no Google, no external model APIs). Your audio, transcripts, and recaps stay on FocusJam’s own GPU cluster. Enterprise customers can go further: deploy the entire stack on their own infrastructure for complete data sovereignty.

Is my video and audio end-to-end encrypted?

Yes. All jam media is end-to-end encrypted. Enterprise customers can bring their own E2EE keys for full key control — meaning FocusJam’s infrastructure never sees plaintext media, even in transit through our relay servers.

Can I self-host FocusJam?

Yes — on the Enterprise plan. FocusJam offers sovereign, self-hosted deployment on your own infrastructure, including OpenZiti ZTNA private networking so jam sessions never touch the public internet. Contact us to scope your deployment.

What is an AI Jammer?

An AI Jammer is FocusJam’s AI meeting participant — it joins every session, listens in real time, takes structured notes, tracks decisions and action items, flags risks, and maintains a live meeting health score. At the end of each jam it produces a full recap: notes, decisions, action items, and risk flags, downloadable as Markdown or JSON. It can also answer questions live during the session.

Can I rename or customize my AI Jammer?

Yes, on Team and Enterprise plans. You can give your AI Jammer a custom name and configure its persona so it feels like a genuine member of your team. Solo plan users get the default Jammer experience with a standard name and behavior.

How does seat-based billing work on the Team plan?

The Team plan is priced per seat (per user account) per month. Each team member with a FocusJam account counts as one seat. You can add or remove seats at any time — changes are prorated to your next billing cycle. Annual plans are billed as a single upfront payment at the discounted per-seat rate.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. Monthly plans can be cancelled at any time — your access continues through the end of the current billing period. Annual plans can be cancelled at renewal. No long-term contracts on Solo or Team.
