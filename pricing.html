FAQ

Common questions, straight answers.

The Free tier lets you run E2EE 1:1 and small-group meetings (up to ~5 participants) with a 40-minute session cap — no credit card required. You get one AI Jammer per session, but it is gated to recap-only output with a single wake trigger and a cap of 5 jammed meetings per month. Recaps are retained for 7 days. Everything runs on FocusJam’s managed sovereign cloud. Upgrade to Solo when you need unlimited meetings, more participants, or the full Jammer feature set.

An AI Jammer is FocusJam’s AI meeting participant. It joins every session, listens in real time, takes structured notes, tracks decisions and action items, flags risks, and maintains a live meeting health score. At the end of each jam it produces a full recap: notes, decisions, action items, and risk flags, downloadable as Markdown or JSON. On paid plans it can also answer questions live during the session. On the Free tier the Jammer is limited to a single wake trigger and recap-only output.

Yes, on Team and Enterprise plans. You can give your AI Jammer a custom name and configure its persona so it feels like a genuine member of your team. Solo plan users get the full Jammer feature set with a standard name and behavior. Free plan users get the default Jammer experience with the recap-only limitation described above.

FocusJam operates its own AI infrastructure — no third-party AI SaaS (no OpenAI, no Google, no external model APIs). Your audio, transcripts, and recaps stay on FocusJam’s own GPU cluster. Enterprise customers can go further: deploy the entire stack on their own infrastructure for complete data sovereignty. No plan ever routes your meeting data through an external AI provider.

Yes, on every plan including Free. All jam media is end-to-end encrypted. Enterprise customers can bring their own E2EE keys for full key control, meaning FocusJam’s infrastructure never sees plaintext media, even in transit through our relay servers.

Yes — on the Enterprise plan. FocusJam offers sovereign, self-hosted and on-prem deployment on your own infrastructure, including OpenZiti ZTNA private networking so jam sessions never touch the public internet. Free, Solo, and Team plans run on FocusJam’s managed sovereign cloud. Contact us to scope your Enterprise deployment.

The Team plan is priced per seat (per user account) per month. Each team member with a FocusJam account counts as one seat. You can add or remove seats at any time — changes are prorated to your next billing cycle. Annual plans are billed as a single upfront payment at the discounted rate ($18/seat/mo, $216/seat/yr). Monthly billing is $24/seat/mo.

Absolutely. Monthly plans can be cancelled at any time — your access continues through the end of the current billing period. Annual plans can be cancelled at renewal. No long-term contracts on Free, Solo, or Team. Enterprise agreements vary; ask your account manager.
